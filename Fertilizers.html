Fertilizers

HERO K44 (13:0:44 + TE)

Hero K is specially produced 100%water soluble fertilizer. It can be applied through fertigation system or as a foliar spray on fruit crops such as Banana, Papaya, Guava, Water Melon, Pineapple & Flowering plants such as Orchid, Roses, Anthurium, Bogaenvila & Carnetion as flowery booster.

Recommended Crops

Orchid, Anthurium, Rose, Boganvila & All foliage, All cut flowers , All fruits and All vegetable cultivations including Carrot, Beetroot,Potato..etc.

Dosage

Flowers & Vegetables :Apply when Flowering stage 45g-50g per 16L tank

Fruit: Apply when Flowering stage 50g-60g per 16L tank



Albert's Solution

Albert’s Solution is a quality product of Grow More Inc. of USA.This balanced fertilizer is 100% water soluble and it contains most of the macro and micro nutrients which is needed for better plant growth. Albert’s Solution can be used in Polytunnel,Hydroponic greenhouse cultivations.This product can be applied as a foliar spray for vegetables,fruits,flowering plants,ornamental plants and other field crops successfully.

Directions for use

For Vegetables,Fruits, Flower and Field Crops -10 grams dilute in 4.5 liters of water and apply as a Foliar Spray. For Soilless – growing media,Greenhouse and Polytunnel,1kg dilute in 450-500 liters of water.
